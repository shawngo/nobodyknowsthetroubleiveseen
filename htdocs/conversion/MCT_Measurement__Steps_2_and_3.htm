Calculating the MCT Measurement: Steps 2 and 3

Step 2 - Order Processing Time

From the Ops Plan Summary Table on your VSM, enter the Order Processing Time. Then enter the work hours per day and the work days per week (e.g. 8 hours, 5 days per week).

 

 

Step 3 - Raw Material Time

From your VSM, enter the time for the Raw Material Inventory on Hand. Then enter the work hours per day and the work days per week (e.g. 8 hours, 5 days per week).  Raw Material uses the same work hours per day and work days per week as the Manufacturing Loop. Kitted Raw Material is included in Raw Material on Hand, not WIP. Time for kitting activities (but not material) is included in the production loop.
