Implementation

Manufacturing Critical-path Time (MCT) reduction is the project’s driving strategy.  MCT reduction will result in reduced waste in many operational areas.  These improvements will be defined and tracked through the Value Stream Mapping process.  Typical waste-related metrics defined on the Value Stream Maps are:

  • Order Processing Time, Raw Material Procurement Time, Raw Material Time

  • Production Cycle Time

  • Production WIP and Queue Time

  • Production Change Over Time

  • Production Batch Wait Time

  • Logistics Time, Material Travel distance

  • Finished Goods Time

  • Production Quality at the Source

Supplier is committed to completion of Phase II MCT Gap closure work within the agreed upon schedule.  MEP assistance is available to support Supplier on this, if needed, due to Supplier resource or technical knowledge limitations.  Supplier is responsible for 100% of MEP fees for Phase II MCT Gap closure assistance.  State subsidization of this work may be available and should be explored with Local MEP personnel.
