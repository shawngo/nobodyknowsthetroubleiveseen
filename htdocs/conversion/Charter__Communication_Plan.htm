Communication Plan

a) The Supplier shall be the sole reporter of project data to the OEM.  The Local MEP and MEP SCA will hold all supplier project data confidential from OEM and other suppliers.

b) The Supplier agrees to share company data with the MEP for project execution and analysis. The Local MEP and MEP SCA will hold all supplier project data confidential from OEM and other suppliers.

c) The Supplier will hold a Phase I and Phase II project report-out meeting with the OEM according to the timetable outlined in charter section 4.

d) The Supplier will hold a Phase I and Phase II project review meeting with the MEP SCA in preparation for their OEM Phase I and Phase II project report-out meetings.

e) Joint MEP SCA – Supplier – OEM meetings may be called at the discretion of the any of the Project Champions to review project status. These may be by phone or face-to-face.
