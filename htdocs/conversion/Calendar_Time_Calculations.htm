Understanding the Calendar Time Calculations

After completing the 14 MCT measurement steps, the following calculations are displayed on the Calendar Time Conversion Sheet:

 

MCT Total: This is the total true lead time for the critical path of the value stream in calendar days in a non-expedited, First In, First Out flow.

 

 

MCT Response: This is a subset of the MCT total, excluding Raw Material and Finished Goods on hand.  It measures the activities associated with order fulfillment. This metric is especially relevant to operational flexibility and order fulfillment capabilities.

 

 

Critical-path Activity Analysis: This compares the amount of "value added activity" to "non-value added activity" relative to the first piece of an order. As in the Value Stream Map, Value Add is taken from the viewpoint of the first single piece.
