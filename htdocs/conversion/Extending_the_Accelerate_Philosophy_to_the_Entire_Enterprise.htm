The MEP Accelerate process and the book by Rajan Suri, It’s About Time, have a common goal:  To introduce manufacturers to the competitive advantage of applying time-based thinking throughout the manufacturing enterprise.  We recommend this book as a complementary resource to the Accelerate process for exposing manufacturing business leaders to the rationale and tactical approaches for applying time-based thinking as a comprehensive strategy.  To promote your further investigation, we’ve created a brief summary of a few critical concepts presented in this book.
