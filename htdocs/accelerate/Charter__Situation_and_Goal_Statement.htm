Situation and Goal Statement

Situation

Supplier is an important partner to OEM.  Increased domestic and global competition necessitates that both Supplier and OEM continually work to increase their overall value propositions in order to remain competitive.  This can be accomplished, in part, through a strategy of Lean Manufacturing.  The U.S. Department of Commerce Manufacturing Extension Partnership Program (MEP) was established to assist small to mid-sized domestic manufacturers implement modern production principles, including Lean Manufacturing.  The MEP Supply Chain Advantage (SCA) is responsible for oversight of the delivery of supply chain improvement services in the MEP network.

Accelerate--- a MEP product developed to facilitate waste reduction through a focus on reducing total product lead-time, i.e. Manufacturing Critical-path Time (MCT)--- has a long track-record of successfully increasing client competitiveness.  The MEP SCA functions as the single point of contact for the OEM for all Accelerate projects and coordinate the supplier and local MEP delivery.

Goal

The goal of this project is to increase Supplier competitiveness and order fulfillment capability through an improvement in total product lead-time, i.e. Manufacturing Critical-path Time (MCT). MCT reduction strongly correlates to improvements in operational effectiveness and increased order fulfillment flexibility. Actions taken to achieve a reduction in MCT will also lead to improvements in quality, on-time delivery and total cost.  Consequently, Accelerate projects are based on identifying and implementing actions that will reduce MCT.  Existing OEM supplier performance goals continue to apply and will be maintained and/or improved as a result of this project.
