Resource Support

a) Supplier will assign a Project Manager who will have overall project responsibility. Additional resources (internal or external) will be applied by Supplier as needed to meet the objectives of all phases of the program.

b) Project implementation will only occur after Project Plans have been approved by Supplier management.  It is not anticipated that Supplier will require significant capital investment in order to achieve the objectives of this project.  If investment is required, they must fit within the existing framework of Supplier’s R.O.I requirements and/or their strategic plan or they will not be included in the MCT Gap closure plan.
